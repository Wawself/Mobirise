About us

We provide high quality, independent expert reports and services to the court.

Established in 1985, we have unrivalled experience and are trusted by leading Personal Injury and Clinical Negligence Solicitors all across the country – our network covers England, Wales, Scotland and the Channel Islands.

Our experts

Our experts include Occupational Therapists, Nurses, Physiotherapists and Speech and Language Therapists, many of whom are Case Managers in their clinical work. All are passionate about identifying and correctly quantifying claimant’s needs and are among the most knowledgeable and current in their field. Being privately owned, we can invest in their training, support their professional development and have systems in place to share knowledge across the whole team.

Our office

Our office is focused on aiding efficiency to ensure work remains proportionate, saving time and expense for our customers and our experts. We utilise over 30 years’ experience to best support you and the running of your case. Last year, 99% of our reports met or exceeded customer deadlines.

Services

We provide you with helpful free services; a reference tool to help you select the most appropriate expert for your case and a free service which allows you to pose a question to, and receive an answer from, our expert team. We also offer deferred payment terms, if required.

Collectively we combine extensive clinical knowledge with innovative commercial solutions to enable more claimants to benefit from our expertise.
